A gentle ridge walk that opens onto a wide, unobstructed sky. Telescopes provided.

  • Duration 2.5 hrs

Bioluminescence Walk

Follow a coastal trail where the tide glows blue underfoot. Best on new-moon nights.

  • Distance 4.0 km
  • Duration 3 hrs

Full Moon Trek

A summit hike timed to the full moon — headlamps optional, moonlight does most of the work.

  • Distance 6.5 km
  • Duration 4 hrs
  • Difficulty Challenging
